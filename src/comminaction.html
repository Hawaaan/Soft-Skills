Soft & Communication Skills

Xirfadaha muhiimka u ah guusha nolosha iyo shaqada

*******************************************************************************************************************************************************************************************

Waa Maxay Soft Skills?

Soft skills waa xirfado shakhsiyadeed sida isgaarsiin, dulqaad, la shaqeyn iyo hogaamin. Waxay ka duwan yihiin xirfadaha farsamo, waxaana lagu bartaa waayo-aragnimo iyo is-dhexgal bulsho.

  • Communication (Isgaarsiin)
  • Teamwork (La Shaqeyn)
  • Problem Solving (Xallinta Dhibaatooyinka)
  • Time Management (Maareynta Waqtiga)
  • Leadership (Hoggaan)
  • Adaptability (La qabsasho)

Waa Maxay Communication Skills?

Communication skills waa awoodda aad u leedahay inaad si cad, hufan, oo faham leh ula xiriirto dadka kale — hadal ahaan, qoraal ahaan, ama hadal la’aan ahaan.

Qaybaha Communication Skills:

1. Verbal (Afka ah)

Hadal toos ah sida kulamada, wareysiyada ama doodaha.

2. Non-Verbal

Luqadda jirka: dhoolacadeyn, istaag toosan, indho eegid.

3. Written

Fariimo qoraal ah sida Email, CV, warbixino.

4. Listening

Inaad si dhab ah u dhageysato dadka kale adigoon ka dhexgalin.

5. Confidence

Hadalku ha noqdo mid kalsooni leh oo cod sugan ah.

Sidee Looga Horumarin Karaa?

  • Ku celceli hadalka dad hortooda (presentation).
  • Dhageyso si dhab ah dadka kale oo su’aalo weydii.
  • Akhri & qor qoraallo si aad u horumariso eraybixinta.
  • Ka shaqee kalsooni iyo dhoola-cadeyn markaad la hadlayso dadka.
